About This Project

This was a pretty simple project for a course where we had to create an informative poster for an existing product.

Interestingly, when we had time to work on this project, my laptop froze, and I couldn't use Photoshop. So, I resorted to using Google Drawings. I knew Google Drawings isn't a professional, high-quality platform, but for this project, the design and layout were what mattered.

I used the masking tool on my Apple iPhone to remove the background from photos I took myself, and I did my best to make Google Drawings work for this assignment. I'm pretty proud of how it turned out, given my resources and time constraints.

You may notice the reference to Jeremy Allen White, which I feel needs explaining. My group in this class decided at the beginning of the semester to somehow reference Jeremy Allen White or his various projects in *all* of our submissions. This extra challenge served as a good icebreaker in class discussions and genuinely challenged us to create more interesting work. It was a great opportunity to put in a little more effort by stretching the rules of the original assignment.
