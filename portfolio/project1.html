when spirits sing EP

This project was my final for an English course during my freshman year. We were given the opportunity to express our learning in any form we pleased. Of course, making music was a no-brainer since it fit perfectly with our class discussions and assignments about music, and it strengthened my musical abilities by writing on specific topics within a short time frame.

As an extra part of this project, I analyzed each song with Genius-style annotations and expanded on the meaning of some lyrics. This allowed me to better understand my own often flow-of-consciousness writing and express what I wanted to say in clearer words. Below are the annotated lyrics.
